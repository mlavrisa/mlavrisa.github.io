About Me

I believe the best engineering starts with deep understanding. Whether in machine learning, AI interpretability, materials science, or hardware prototyping, I approach problems through first principles and hands-on iteration. My work has spanned startups, scientific research, and product development—always with a focus on clarity, efficiency, and building systems that work in the real world. When I'm not designing or prototyping, I'm thinking about how technology shapes the future—or questioning the assumptions we take for granted.
